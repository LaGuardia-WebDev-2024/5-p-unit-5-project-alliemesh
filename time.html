Thomas Paine Timeline

1737

Thomas Paine born in Thetford, England, on January 29th (one day after my birthday!)

1774

Meets Benjamin Franklin and leaves England for North America

1775

Becomes editor of the Pennsylvania Magazine and starts writing pro-independence essays.

1776

Publishes his most famous work, the pamphlet, "Common Sense."

1776-1777

Writes a series called "American Crisis," inspiring soldiers to fight the Revolutionary War.

1787

Returns to Europe, mainly France and England

1791-1792

Publishes "The Rights of Man", helping the French Revolution

1793

Imprisoned in France for participating in the French Revolution

1794

Released from prison

1802

Returns to United States after being criticized and opposed in Europe

1809

Dies in New York City on June 8th
